Different Words and abbreviations

BG (BGing)

BG means Background Fighting which means fighting in the background while the main battle is happening. When someone says "You're bging", it means you are fighting in the background. BG often presents as a negative meaning because you are usually not allowed to bg at trainings/tryouts.

P2W

P2W means "Pay to Win" which means you pay real money to get previlligies in the game that others don't have if they didn't buy it. It is strictly prohibited to use in any events inside the clan except for raids, snipers or even sometimes wars..

qed

qed meams (quarantined) which often refers in a form "You will get qed" or "I got qed". It can also be "quaraed" like "why do you try to quara him" or "why did he get unquaraed?".

EU, NA

EU stands for Europe and NA stands for North America. It is being used in tryouts, trainings and other events where region is mandatory to know the ping so the event is comfortable for all attendees

ft

ft means "first to" e.g. ft5 means first-to-5 which means, first one to get 5 points in a fight, wins. Ft also has 3 characters: Garou, Saitama and Metal bat. In ft battles ping abuse techs or m1 resets or bug abuses, are prohibited.

KOS

KOS means Kill On Sight which means, everyone listed in KOS, if seen, to be eliminated/killed immidiately without hesitation. Before killing make sure they are not in Xraid with fake user and not in KOS, otherwise you will be punished for in-clan teaming..
